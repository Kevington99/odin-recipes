Mickey Waffles

A classic park breakfast treat

Makes about 8 Mickey waffles. Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 medium green pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a medium sized mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Whisk to combine
  2. In a separate bowl, combine eggs, buttermilk, vegetable oil and vanilla extract.
  3. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir just until combined
  4. Measure 1/2 cup of batter onto Mickey Mouse waffle maker. Cook for about 3 minutes, or until golden brown
